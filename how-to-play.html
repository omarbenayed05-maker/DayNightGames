How to Play

A quick guide for players

1

Join the room

Get the 4-digit room code from your host, enter it along with your name, and wait in the lobby until the host starts.

2

Check your secret role

Once the game starts, you'll see a face-down card. Press and hold it to peek at your role — release to hide it again. Don't let others see your screen!

3

Daytime discussion

During the day, talk with the group out loud — accuse, defend, and try to figure out who has which role. You can still hold your card to double check your role.

4

Vote

When the host opens voting, tap the name of the player you want to eliminate. You can change your vote as many times as you like until the host confirms.

5

The reveal

Whoever is eliminated has their role revealed to everyone. If you're eliminated, you can keep watching and listening — just no more talking or voting.

6

Nighttime

Everyone closes their eyes. The host quietly calls on certain roles to secretly act. If your role is called, follow whatever action your card describes.

7

Repeat until someone wins

The game cycles between night and day until one side achieves its win condition — your host will explain the specific roles and win conditions for this pack before you start.

💡 Tip: keep your phone's brightness down and your screen tilted away from others while peeking at your card!
